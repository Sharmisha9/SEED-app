Motivations

Nowadays, there is a lot of scientific data that shows the alarming speed of global warming and desertification across the globe, even though we have advanced technology such as the James Webb Telescope that can find water and other chemical elements from hundreds and thousands of light years away. Our project “SEED” seems trivial, but we believe that it is a life and planet-saving project that will solve the eminent problem that our planet and humanity face.
